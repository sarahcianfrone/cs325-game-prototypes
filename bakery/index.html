Bakery Idle v0.1

  • Originality: Having different categories for units to buy gives a lot more choice to the player than in many idle games, where you're just going from one tier to another tier to another.
  • Technical Merit: I use a large number of buttons and sprites to make the tabs and button system work properly.
  • Prototype Postmortem: The hardest part was probably balancing all the numbers, which didnt end up too well. There are so many numbers to balance and it made it really difficult to create a good balance of difficulty.
  • Prototype Assets: I made most of my assets from scratch. The one exception is the purple background, which my girlfriend made (Thanks Anna).
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: It's similar to a lot of other idle games like Cookie Clicker and others.
